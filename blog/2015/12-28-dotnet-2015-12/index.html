.NET in December 2015

We are targeting Microsoft .NET in our search. We see Microsoft as the biggest and most important software vendor in the information worker market (people working with office software suits) and also market dominant in PC operating systems.

Microsoft also had kind-of bad reputation before, but it changed a lot in last year and for example has opened .NET and created cross-platform CoreCLR.

.NET allows you to target both people using PCs at office work, people using supported Unix variants at work and also servers.
